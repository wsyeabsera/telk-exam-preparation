# 14-Day Study Schedule / 14-Tage-Lernplan

**Goal:** Focus on your weak points (Dativ, adjective endings, prepositions, word order) and practise all four exam sections before the exam.  
**Recommendation:** Study every day; mix grammar drills, vocabulary, and exam tasks.

## Daily Routine Suggestion

1. **Warm-up (5 min):** Quick look at 00_Quick_Reference (one cheatsheet).  
2. **Main (30–45 min):** Lesson + exercises from the day’s materials.  
3. **Extra (optional):** 10–15 min vocabulary from 06_Vocabulary_By_Topic or 07_Exam_Skills/speaking_phrases.

**Deutsche Erklärung:** Jeden Tag zuerst die Schnellübersicht, dann die Tagesmaterialien mit Übungen. Optional Vokabeln oder Redemittel.

---

## Day Before the Exam

- No heavy new material.  
- Skim 00_Quick_Reference (dativ_cheatsheet, common_mistakes, preposition_master_list).  
- Get enough sleep.

**Deutsche Erklärung:** Am Tag vor der Prüfung nur wiederholen, nicht neu lernen. Ausruhen.
